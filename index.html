Speedometer & Trails

The ultimate companion for your rides. Track your speed, map your journey, and now, analyze every turn with advanced lean angle metrics.

Advanced Lean Angle Tracking

Experience your ride like never before with our new real-time lean angle gauge. The app tracks your every move, displaying your current lean angle and remembering your maximums for each ride. Recalibrate anytime with a tap or automatically when your heading changes.

Dynamic Color Trails

Your location trail is now a vibrant reflection of your ride's intensity. The path shifts from green to red as you lean into turns (0-45°), and to a deep purple for extreme angles (45-90°), creating a beautiful and insightful map of your journey.

Real-Time Speed Display

Get your current speed displayed with pinpoint accuracy. Your current location is marked in red, with color-coded trails showing where you've been. Tap the speed to instantly switch between km/h and mph.

Custom Speed Alerts

Set lower and upper speed limits with a convenient dual-handle slider. The app will alert you if you go above or below your set range. Easily switch between slow and fast speed ranges for precise control.

Favorites & Trail Management

Keep track of where you've been with a visual trail. Save up to 3 favorite locations for easy reference. Want to start fresh? Clear your trail and max lean angles with a single tap.
