Speedometer and Trails

Real-Time Speed Display

Get your current speed displayed on your iPhone with pinpoint accuracy. Your current location is marked in red, with blue trails showing where you have been. Tap on the speed to switch between kmph or mph.

Set Speed Limits

Set lower and upper speed limits with the Low and High buttons, beyond which the app will give an alert. Adjust the limits with a convenient slider featuring two grab points. The slider range can be changed by tapping on the icons at the right of the slider so that it is easy to select slower or faster limits.

Historic Location Trail and Favorites

Keep track of where you've been with a visual trail of your historic locations. Save up to 3 favorite locations for easy navigation by using the three pin buttons at the bottom. The favorite locations stay saved until you save a new location in the same colored button.

Clear Your Trail

Want to start fresh? Clear your trail with just a click to start a clean slate.
